RMS Buddy

RMS Buddy is a handy tool for tracking RMS and peak volume information. It gives you independent read-outs for left and right channels.

The average RMS keeps a cumulative, running average RMS of the audio signal. It can be reset by pressing its reset button.

The continual RMS is the RMS at the current moment (representing the current 70 ms snippet of sound approximately).

Absolute peak displays the highest peak volume level detected so far for each channel. Pressing its reset button will make it start looking for the highest peak again.

Continual peak displays the highest peak volume level at the current moment (representing the current 70 ms snippet of sound approximately).
